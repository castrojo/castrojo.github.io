![JAM
TIME!](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuGlobalJam?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=ugj09_button_orange_250x148_en.png)

It’s that time again folks, [sign up your
LoCo](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuGlobalJam/Events). “But Jorge, your
LoCo isn’t on there either!” you say, well, that’s because this is the
time we start to plan and looking for venues, finding out what kind of
jam you want to do and start preparations for the weekend of 26-28
March.

This would also be a good time for your LoCo to find if there’s a
[hackerspace](http://hackerspaces.org/wiki/) in your area and get
involved in their development. Also, this is the first time we’re having
… [upgrade jams](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Jams/Upgrade), where people can
concentrate on the upgrade experience.

If you have questions I’m in \#ubuntu-locoteams!
