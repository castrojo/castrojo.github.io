    Winter and Hockey for Now

    It’s been about a year since we moved down to Boca Raton, Florida, almost to the day.

    Back then juju was called Ensemble, and I had just started my new role at Canonical doing cloud related stuff. Now that my amazing wife has finished her doctorate degree I can ~~retire~~ follow her wherever she finds a gig that’s best for her. So this week we’ve packed our bags to head back to Michigan; where she starts a new position at the Hygeia Center for the healing arts.

    … and off we go to Ann Arbor, Michigan. Unfortunately for me, as a Michigan State Spartan this doesn’t bode well. Oh well, I’ll have to represent. Besides, Ann Arbor is not just home to a great paleontogy museum, the tech community in A2 is probably the epicenter of technology in Michigan, with really strong Python and Ruby communitites. And let’s not forget about the local microbrews.

    Man I will miss the weather, latin food, and sheer lazy attitude of Florida, and head back to dingy Michigan with its rust, crappy winters, and Kid Rock.

    Florida’s been fun. It’s been great to see the Florida Local Team in action, and especially visting Planet Linux Caffe. Being in such proximity to my native Puerto Rico has let my visit the island twice, I got to see a dolphin with her football-sized cub in the wild while snaking my way through the everglades, I’ve hit up the zoos in Miami and West Palm Beach, there’s some really great wildlife out here if you’re into that kind of thing. I’ll really miss the Blue Herons that congregate right outside my window every day. :-/

    I won’t miss almost getting hit by some old lady in her Mercedes on the way to CVS to get pop every other day though. Seriously.

    We’re hitting the road on Friday, check me on latitude if you want to follow along. My wife has planned us to hit a bunch of restaurants featured on Diners, Drive-ins, and Dives the entire way up (see, I told you she was amazing).
