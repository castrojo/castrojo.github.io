<div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">

Thanks to the bot-like Fabien Tassin there are now Chromium builds of
the “Beta Channel” the recent Chrome is based off of.

<https://launchpad.net/~chromium-daily/+archive/beta/>

fta’s original PPA was tracking the Chromium builds every day. So every
day it would build new debs. Depending on what landed in upstream
Chromium this could be good or bad. This specific PPA is of the beta
channel, so it is the upstream that Google Chrome Beta is built off of,
as such it’s not updated every day, just what lands in the more tested
Beta Channel.

*If you want to use Chromium but not experience the churn of the dailies
this is the build you want.* If you don’t like the
auto-updating-I-put-something-in-your-sources.list.d .deb Google puts
out and prefer a non-branded experience then this is what you want.
