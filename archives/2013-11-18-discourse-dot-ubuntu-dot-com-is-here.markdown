<div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">

After about \~7 months of prototyping our
[watercooler](http://www.jorgecastro.org/2013/05/31/reinvigorate-the-water-cooler/)
is here.

Introducing [discourse.ubuntu.com](http://discourse.ubuntu.com):

![](http://www.jorgecastro.org/images/new_discourse.png)

Our new discussion site built on the modern 100% Free Software
[Discourse platform](http://discourse.org). Log in with your Ubuntu SSO
and start the party!

Over the next week or so I’ll be detailing instructions for community
members to integrate their blog comments with the platform, and we have
a bunch of integration ideas between Discourse and
[summit.ubuntu.com](http://summit.ubuntu.com) , as well as other
integration points with mailing lists and social networks.

We also have an [entire
section](http://discourse.ubuntu.com/category/local-teams) dedicated to
Local Team categories, so if you’re looking to jump in with your local
team, let me know.
