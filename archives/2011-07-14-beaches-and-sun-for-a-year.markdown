Next week I’m packing up my things and moving to Boca Raton, Florida,
home of awesome beaches and retired people who sit around.

It’s going to be a busy Summer, I’ve started working on
[Ensemble](https://ensemble.ubuntu.com/) this cycle so you’ll see me
talking about that a bunch more – it’s nice to be working with
server-type stuff again, especially with something new and exciting.

![](http://media.tumblr.com/tumblr_lobrdo3pcX1qb5bmy.jpg)

I look forward to becoming part of the [Florida
team](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/FloridaTeam)!

Unfortunately my schedule this summer will mean I won’t be able to go to
Debconf and the Desktop Summit this year, which is a bummer as they’ve
both become my favorite events with great friends. Not to worry though,
[Jason Warner](http://jasoncwarner.posterous.com/) has settled in as
manager of the Canonical desktop team and will be at the Desktop Summit
to ~~buy you beer~~ field any questions you might have.

There’ll be plenty of Ubuntu people at Debconf as well, hopefully I’ll
be able to attend next year.
