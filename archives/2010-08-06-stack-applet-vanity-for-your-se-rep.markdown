![](http://media.tumblr.com/tumblr_l6qgdf4qWE1qb5bmy.png)

Here’s a nice little application that will tell you what your reputation
is on any site on [Stack Exchange](http://stackexchange.com/sites),
including [Ubuntu](http://ubuntu.stackexchange.com/).

I mailed George Edison (the author) with some tips and stuff, and next
thing you know he’s got it up [on
Launchpad](https://edge.launchpad.net/stackapplet) ready to go, so if
you want to dive in and start filing and fixing bugs, feel free to hop
in.

The team PPA isn’t ready yet, so use George’s PPA to install it:

**ppa:george-edison55/george-edison**

The package name is “stackapplet”. Many thanks to Luke Faraone for
uploading this into Debian (it’ll make it’s way into Maverick as well!).
