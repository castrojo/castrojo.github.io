So I love covers, in fact, I love the
[covers](http://www.last.fm/tag/covers) station on last.fm. I love
pseudo-covers too, songs that are clearly based on something else, but
you just can’t quite catch what. So like, Weezer’s sweater song is
[basically](http://www.chartattack.com/news/73880/weezer-ripped-off-metallica-get-online-radio-station)
Metallica’s Sanitarium. Duh.

One of my favorites is Sum 41’s [The Bitter
End](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GlE4t89lgvs). So instead of doing a
Metallica cover, they just basically rewrite the song and throw out
something new as an homage. I dig it, especially since “Battery” is one
of my favorite songs. I love trying to figure these out.

I am digging Disturbed’s “Land of Confusion” lately, and I’m sure those
of you that have been to UDS have heard Jono play Children of Bodom’s
“Oops I did it again”. Yes, [it
exists](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D1sguF2D1UA).

What are your favorite covers? Have a good weekend!
