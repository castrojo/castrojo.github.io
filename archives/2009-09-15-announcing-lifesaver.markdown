<div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">

Chris Jones, creator of [Terminator](http://www.tenshu.net/terminator/)
has been working on a small little tool that you might like. It’s called
[Lifesaver](http://www.tenshu.net/archives/2009/09/14/new-project-released-lifesaver/).
It’s a small screensaver that searches for “ubuntu” on twitter and
identi.ca and then presents it on your screen all slick-like. Here’s
what it looks like on my dual monitors:
!\[img*0925\](http://castrojo.files.wordpress.com/2009/09/img*0925.jpg)

Cute huh? Chris has a PPA for Karmic
[here](https://launchpad.net/~cmsj/+archive/lifesaver). Unfortunately
the version of goocanvas in Jaunty is too old to do what he needed to do
to get a PPA working for Jaunty. So if you’re feeling like contributing,
you might want to [check out the
code](https://code.launchpad.net/lifesaver) and see if you can make it
work on Jaunty to give Chris a hand, patches in this area are very
welcome.
