<div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">

Spent some time checking out the Ubuntu booth at FOSDEM, run by the
folks from the Ubuntu [Belgian
Team](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BelgianTeam):

[![](http://lh6.google.com/jorge.castro/R8Taq2ca05I/AAAAAAAAEf8/5iAZAWHvcRg/s288/IMG_0399.JPG)](http://picasaweb.google.com/jorge.castro/FOSDEM2008/photo#5171498701832246162)

Thanks to the many folks (some not in the picture) who helped man the
booth and answer user questions. Having run many booths myself I know it
can be tiring even after a few hours, let alone two whole days. They
also put together an *awesome* little poster for their upcoming release
party:

[![](http://lh4.google.com/jorge.castro/R8TapWca03I/AAAAAAAAEfg/bDa7uMWzjv4/s288/IMG_0397.JPG)](http://picasaweb.google.com/jorge.castro/FOSDEM2008/photo#5171498676062442354)

Of course they will make the source available so that other locos can
reuse the layout for making their own release party invitations. I will
blog as soon as someone sends it to me.
