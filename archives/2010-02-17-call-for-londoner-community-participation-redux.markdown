Live in the London UK, area and looking to help Ubuntu? The Canonical
design team is looking for volunteers to attend research sessions in
London. The dates available are 22 Feb through 5 March. This time around
we are looking for technically savvy users, you don’t need to be a
sysadmin but you should probably know your PPAs and GPGs. The session
takes approximately one hour and there will be a cash incentive.

Please email canonical.research@googlemail.com if you can participate
and someone from the research agency will get in touch with you.

I have created [a wiki
page](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/CanonicalUserResearch) for all upcoming
testing so if you’re in the area and interested please subscribe to the
page!
