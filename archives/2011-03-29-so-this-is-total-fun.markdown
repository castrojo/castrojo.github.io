<div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">

One of my friends has a [Syma S107](http://syma107.com/) Micro RC
helicopter and when I heard these are about \$30-\$40 I decided to snag
a
[Z008](http://www.amazon.com/Z008-4ch-Co-axial-Helicopter-Gyro/dp/B004FLMT8Q),
which is similar but adds these side fan things so I can slide side to
side: 

![](https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/_1Thkya8n93k/TZJPqYTObiI/AAAAAAAAftA/cqUpbJzvNVc/s400/IMG_20110329_173028.jpg)

It’s a bunch of fun to fly inside, and very beginner friendly. I hear
they’re setting up a course at a local hackerspace so people can do
races, etc. I was getting off easy the first week but today I broke a
tail rotor and a main blade, luckily it came with spare parts and
they’re pretty hackable.

Anyway I had no idea RC helicopters had gotten so easy to fly and cheap,
it only took a little while to get going, though I’m going to need more
room, heh.
