Hi everyone,

Ted and Cody have been busy this week with non-appmenu related business,
so the team has decided to skip a release this week since there hasn’t
been much work done on the menu.  The team is aware of the major issues,
like “some apps are slow to draw the menu” like Nautilus, the Epiphany
“I think I want to draw the menu but not really” bookmark bug, and the
completion of the desktop menu, which continues to elude Ted, and the
occasional odd bug. Don’t fret, the menu is is great shape considering
we’re not even at alpha 3 yet and we’re heading into a team sprint next
week. It’s likely we’ll shut off the double menus next week and be
feature complete.

As always, here’s the latest commits:

<https://code.launchpad.net/indicator-appmenu>\
<https://code.launchpad.net/appmenu-gtk>

And overall instructions for the
project: https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DesktopExperienceTeam/ApplicationMenu

As always feel free to contact me if you have issues.
