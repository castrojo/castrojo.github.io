<div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">

Whit, Chuck, and Matt are at it again. This “New Workloads Team” has
been busy collecting all the hot container technology stacks from around
the Open Source space and bringing them hot to you. Docker, etcd, kubes,
flannel; all that hot stuff, all nice and bundle-ized for you.

Today the [Kubernetes Project](http://kubernetes.io) is releasing 1.0,
and we’ve got everything you need to get a cluster up and running on any
of your clouds, as well as baremetal.

[Here are the
instructions](http://insights.ubuntu.com/2015/07/21/introducing-kubernetes-version-1-0/),
feel free to kick on them and let us know how it works for you! You’ll
find us on \#juju and \#google-containers on Freenode.
