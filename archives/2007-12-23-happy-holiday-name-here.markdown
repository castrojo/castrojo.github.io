Time for some quickies.

-   Our friends at [Aperture Labs](http://www.aperturescience.com/) have
    a great holiday message.

-   Enjoy [Ubuntu 8.04 Alpha
    2](http://www.ubuntu.com/testing/hardy/alpha2).

-   I gave up and finally signed up for
    [twitter](http://twitter.com/whiprush).

-   Anyone else finding Facebook becoming a pain in the ass chore to
    visit? I don’t want to be a vampire, pirate, or a ninja. I just want
    to keep in touch with my friends. FFS.

-   People have wondered how
    [Simplifymedia](http://stompbox.typepad.com/blog/2007/12/simplify.html)
    differs from something like
    [Firefly](http://www.fireflymediaserver.org/). The key difference
    here being that Simplifymedia works over the internet. My username
    is whiprush btw if you want to friend me.

-   Bazaar is now [1.0](http://bazaar-vcs.org/). If you’ve had
    performance problems with bzr in the past, now is the time to revist
    bzr, I think you’ll be pleasantly surprised at the improvements.
    Congrats to the bzr team!

-   I am really liking [Mozilla
    Weave](http://labs.mozilla.com/2007/12/introducing-weave/). I have
    it on my laptop and my desktop and I have synced goodness. A much
    better implementation than the google sync thing.

-   I have become addicted to
    [Smallville](http://www.cwtv.com/shows/smallville) on HDNet. I think
    it’s interesting to get all this back story on Supes. It’s WAY too
    corny, but I love it anyway.

-   Iain’s [sense of
    humor](http://blogs.gnome.org/iain/2007/12/23/i-value-my-privacy/)
    always makes me laugh.

-   If you’re in the midwest US, make sure you register for
    [penguicon](http://penguicon.org/), while it’s still cheap!

-   Banshee trunk is your Jesus.

Merry Christmas to everyone! (Except for DrPizza, who doesn’t believe in
Christmas, because he’s a heathen.)
