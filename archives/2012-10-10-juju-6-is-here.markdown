<div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">

Clint’s just [pushed Juju
v0.6](https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/juju/2012-October/001932.html)
into Quantal and the Release PPA.

While the OpenStack and MAAS updates are nice, I’m really looking
forward to the [new Local
Provider](https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/juju/2012-October/001922.html),
which has been refactored to use Ubuntu’s Cloud Images instead of a
chroot/debootstrap. Now you get the *exact* image locally as you do in
the cloud, which means an even closer tie between local development and
what you deploy.

All these goodies are in [the
PPA](https://launchpad.net/~juju/+archive/pkgs) and you can [get started
here](https://juju.ubuntu.com/docs/getting-started.html)
