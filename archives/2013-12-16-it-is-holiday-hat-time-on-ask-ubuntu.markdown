The following is a guest post from [Lucio
Martinez](https://luciommartinez.wordpress.com/):

Yesterday the community of Ask Ubuntu has joined to all the Stack
Exchange network [celebrating the
holidays](http://blog.stackoverflow.com/2013/12/winter-bash-2013-is-here/)
in the the coolest way, by *wearing hats*!

Those that had been visiting the site one year ago were happy when they
started to see a new bonus for doing cool stuff like *editing,
answering, etc*. This year you get a bonus. Hats, and here I’m going to
show you why they are so amazing:

![](http://www.jorgecastro.org/images/mateo.png){.src}

Now you know *perfectly* why hats are great :)

The hats have [their own page](http://winterbash2013.stackexchange.com/)
where you can see them all and also the action to unlock it. For
instance, someone who won “The Stallman” for *edit a community wiki
post* will get this:

![](http://www.jorgecastro.org/images/thestallman.png){.src}

So start editing, answering, upvoting, chating, reviewing and doing all
those amazing things that you do every day to make this a great
community!

*LET THE HATS BEGIN!*
