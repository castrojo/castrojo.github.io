During Ubuntu Developer Week Daniel and I [had a
session](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MeetingLogs/devweek1001/AdoptUpstream)
on how to work better with upstreams.

Part of this work has been [improving
documentation](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Upstream) on how upstreams can
find help with what they need, and on the other side of the coin
empowering our contributors to be more useful to upstreams. One of these
projects is [Adopt A
Package](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BugSquad/AdoptPackage), where a bug
person can take ownership of a package and work specifically on
improving it’s situation.

Here’s the [page for
Nautilus](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BugSquad/AdoptPackage/Nautilus).
You’ll note that the team is also keeping a log of the work they’re
doing. If you have a favorite package that you would like to adopt,
consider [joining the
team](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BugSquad/AdoptPackage)!

If you have more questions, Sense Hofstede and I will be running
sessions on \#ubuntu-classroom next month (11 March and 18 March) to
share tips and tricks! See the [Classroom
Schedule](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Classroom) for more details.
