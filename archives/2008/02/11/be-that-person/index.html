    Be That Person.

    Daniel Holbach recently blogged about Ubuntu Developer Week.

    We have a Full Week of developer oriented programming coming up, and I just want to reiterate a few things I’ve talked to about to people over the years:

    • You can do this.

    • Ubuntu is a community-run project, if you have ideas, and want to get involved, then this is the perfect “on-ramp” to get involved in development.

    • We have experts standing by: On top of this upcoming we week, we have regularly scheduled events where we answer questions and generally help develop your skills.

    • Our crack team of MOTUs have a list of bitesize bugs, for those of you who are experienced enough to handle easy-to-fix bugs.

    • Debdiffs are an awesome way to fix those kind of bugs, catch Daniel on Tuesday to learn how to make one.

    • Check out how to be involved in a hug day to fix bugs

    If you’re looking to get involved, then work off this list … I know that for sure there are people out there running hardy always wondering how to make Ubuntu better, now is your chance; come get some.

    And if you can’t do that, at least digg it. :)
