Next Wednesday, 31 March, 2000UTC (That’s 4pm EST) in
\#ubuntu-classroom. We’re going to go over what it means to
[Adopt-a-Package](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BugSquad/AdoptPackage). These
are stalwart individuals that work with upstreams to keep the juices
flowing by filtering out bad bug reports, forwarding good ones, making
sure patches flow upstream, and generally be “My person at Ubuntu” for
upstreams that might not be familiar with how we work.

As you can see on the page, some people have [stepped
up](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BugSquad/AdoptPackage) to the plate, but
with like all things we could use a hand!
