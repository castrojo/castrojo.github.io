And they say Nautilus is lacking a [time
machine](http://www.apple.com/macosx/features/timemachine.html).

So I spent a great deal of time afterhours this UDS with [Dave
Camp](http://www.campd.org/), who before working on Firefox had spent a
great deal of time working on Nautilus, the file manager for GNOME. We
were arguing about Metallica albums or something when someone said
something like this: *(paraphrased conversation follows - guessing who
said what is up to the reader)*

“Well, we can agree to disagree.” “At least Kirk is back.” “Yeah.” “Man,
at least we’re not arguing about f\*\*\*ing spatial Nautilus.” “Hahaha
dude, I miss those days.” “I don’t.” “Want a beer and then go butcher an
[AC/DC
song](http://people.ubuntu.com/%7Ekirkland/Ubuntu_Allstars/2008-12/mp3/09%20-%20You%20Shook%20Me.mp3)?”
“Yeah ….”

Other people apparently [miss those
days](https://www.redhat.com/archives/fedora-devel-list/2008-December/msg02089.html)
too. I suspect people will be arguing about this until the heat death of
the universe.
