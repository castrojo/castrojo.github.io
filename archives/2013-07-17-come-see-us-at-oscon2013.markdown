Just a quick reminder that Mark Mims and I will be at OSCON to talk
about [Service Orchestration In the Cloud With
Juju](http://www.oscon.com/oscon2013/public/schedule/detail/29115) at
9am on Tuesday.

If you’re deploying in the cloud then you should stop by, we’ll be
deploying all sorts of things on HP Cloud.

If you’re around on Monday night for the [Birds of a Feather
sessions](http://www.oscon.com/oscon2013/public/schedule/detail/29595)

OpenStack in production and in general have a discussion on how we use
OpenStack to help drive Ubuntu forward.

Hope to see you there!
