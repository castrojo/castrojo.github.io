Three new plugins for your pleasure:

The first is the pastebin plugin, which is now part of the [official
plugins](https://code.edge.launchpad.net/~do-plugins/gc/do-plugins). You
select text with your mouse, then invoke Do with -space, type “selected
text” (it will autcomplete), then hit tab and select “Send to pastebin”,
then hit enter. Do will submit the text to the pastebin and then return
it to you for you to paste into irc.

Peter Stuifzand’s [ssh
plugin](http://peterstuifzand.nl/gnome-do-plugin-for-connecting-to-ssh.html)
does exactly what you think it would do, let you to connect to remote
servers with ssh quickly:

\[\[![Do\_2\](http://stompbox.typepad.com/blog/images/2008/02/03/do\_2.png)](http://stompbox.typepad.com/photos/uncategorized/2008/02/03/do_2.png)

\](http://stompbox.typepad.com/photos/uncategorized/2008/02/03/do.png)

And lastly, Jacob Andreas’ [twitter
plugin](http://jacobandreas.net/2008/gnome-do-twitter-plugin/), for all
your microblogging needs.
