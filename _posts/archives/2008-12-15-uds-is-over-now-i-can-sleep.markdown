<div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">

There’s this few day window after an [Ubuntu Developer
Summit](http://wiki.ubuntu.com) that reminds me of what it must be like
to be a zombie in a George Romero movie. The flight was a blur, the past
2 days have been a blur, and though I am at Inbox Zero there are a bunch
of things in TODO lists that will take up a great deal of time. I took
the day off and have a few more days lumped around the weekends for
maximum effect. Tomorrow I will begin the long road to organizing the
excellent conversations we had at FOSSCamp and UDS. If there’s something
burning that needs attention then a kick on irc or mail would be
appreciated.

In the meantime I am taking this time to move to wordpress at
<http://castrojo.wordpress.com> - it’s not that I dislike Typepad (which
is hosted Movable Type), it’s just that there’s this culture around
wordpress that I have been missing. I’ve used Typepad for about 3 years
and the service has been nothing but excellent, it’s just weird being
the only person in the room not being involved in a wordpress 2.7
discussion. Heh.
