<div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">

Pulse is not only an excellent live set by Pink Floyd, it’s also a good
name for the idea that the guys behind [pulse of open
source](http://pulseofopensource.com/) came up with. Actually I don’t
know if it’s their idea, but it’s pretty cool.
[Twitter](http://www.twitter.com) is to blogs what IM is to email, or so
they say (I guess).

Pulse of Open Source is sort of like Planet, but for twitter. One random
day [Christer Edwards](http://ubuntu-tutorials.com/) said something
along the lines of “we should organize all Ubuntu people on twitter and
put that to good use.”

Poor Christer made the mistake of having a great open ended idea, so I
convinced him to implement it. :)

[Pulse of Ubuntu](http://pulseofubuntu.tweetpeek.com/) is a twitter
account that follows people around from the Ubuntu community. As you can
see, there’s already a [good list of
people](http://pulseofubuntu.tweetpeek.com/who/) being followed. So if
you use twitter and want to check out what people are doing, then follow
@pulseofubuntu.

If you use twitter and want to microblog about Ubuntu-stuff, then let us
know and you can pulse with us. Send spam to jorge (at) ubuntu dot com
and christer.edwards (at) ubuntu dot com. EDIT: Don’t forget to tell us
your twitterID.

Sign up and follow along, we want to see what [5
bugs](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/5-A-Day) you’re touching every day!
