Wow, it’s been a while since I’ve posted some quickes, let’s get to
work:

-   The Ubuntu [Global Bug Jam](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/GlobalBugJam) is
    this weekend! Sign up your Local Team today! (Where are you Ohio?)

-   Unfortunately I won’t be able to participate in the GBJ, I’ll be on
    a plane to Mar Del Plata to spend some time with our Debian friends
    at [DebConf 8](http://debconf8.debconf.org/).

-   It’s not too late to join [identi.ca](http://identi.ca). Feel free
    to follow me, (I’m @jorge). I try to post Ubuntu related things
    during the day. You can also check out who [I
    follow](http://identi.ca/jorge) if you want to read entries from
    people throughout the Ubuntu and OSS communities.

-   @evan does an excellent job keeping the identi.ca community informed
    of what’s going on in laconica and identi.ca itself - a real welcome
    change from other, well known, less reliable microblogging services.
    Check out his video [here](http://www.linux.com/feature/142676) for
    more details.

-   Ars Technica’s Ryan Paul has been working on
    [Gwibber](http://www.launchpad.net/gwibber), which is a client for
    microblogging services. The current trunk branch is rather boring,
    but the [webkit
    branch](https://code.edge.launchpad.net/%7Esegphault/gwibber/webkitui)
    has some sexiness in it. [Mike
    Basinger](http://www.mikesplanet.net/) has posted a nice [cheat
    sheet](http://www.mikesplanet.net/2008/08/installing-gwibber-webkitui-cheat-sheet/)
    to get you started.

-   Ryan however has been
    [busy](http://arstechnica.com/authors.ars/segphault) covering
    Linuxworld - it would be nice if someone where to start packaging up
    Gwibber in a PPA. \#gwibber on irc.arstechnica.com for
    more information.

-   Barton George from Sun [points
    out](http://blogs.sun.com/barton808/entry/openjdk_default_java_runtime_in)
    that OpenJDK6 is now in main for Intrepid. Now is the time to join
    the Ubuntu [Java Team](http://daniel.holba.ch/blog/?p=132). Thanks
    go out to everyone involved in the OpenJDK and IcedTea projects.

-   Albums I’m listening to with great frequency recently: Disturbed’s
    *Indestructible* and Tool’s *10,000 Days*

-   Of course, everyone knows that [Banshee
    1.2](http://gburt.blogspot.com/2008/07/banshee-12-released.html)
    is out. Jump up, jump and get down!

-   Kirkland is not just the name of the house brand at Costco, on the
    side he also works on [encrypted private
    directories](http://dustinkirkland.wordpress.com/2008/08/06/encrypted-private-directories-in-ubuntu-intrepid/).

-   Thanks for the responses to [Arroz con
    Pollo](http://stompbox.typepad.com/blog/2008/08/arroz-con-pollo.html),
    it helped me find [Open Source
    Food](http://www.opensourcefood.com/).

-   … and lastly, don’t forget to snag your creatively inclined friends
    to participate in the [Ubuntu Free Culture
    Showcase](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuFreeCultureShowcase).

Wow, that’s a bunch of quickies!
