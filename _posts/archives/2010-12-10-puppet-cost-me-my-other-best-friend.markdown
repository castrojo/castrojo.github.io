Now it’s not deja vu. This [happened to me
before](http://castrojo.tumblr.com/post/738588435/puppet-cost-me-my-best-friend).
My other best friend is now also moving to California this month to work
for a large company who is deploying tons of Puppet. It’s a tremendous
opportunity for him, just like the last guy.

Best of luck to him, it’s always good to see projects like Puppet
flourish and for Ken to have an opportunity like this. So as a
goodbye/good luck we went to go see the Wings play the Sharks at the
Joe. Unfortunately for him his home team will now be the San Jose
Sharks, heh.
