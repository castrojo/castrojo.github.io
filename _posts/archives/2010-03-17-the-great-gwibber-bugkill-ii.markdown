The [first
one](http://castrojo.wordpress.com/2010/02/22/the-great-gwibber-bugkill/)
was great, so we’re bringing it back tomorrow. This time we’ve got Pedro
helping us out, and he’s got a nice [target list of
bugs](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuBugDay/20100318) we’re going to
concentrate on. Basically grab a bug, try to resolve it or replicate it,
mark it accordingly, and move on. Refer to the [triage
guide](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Bugs/HowToTriage) for more info, or feel
free to ask on \#ubuntu-bugs on freenode.

We could also use people banging on gwibber in general. Feel free to
[fire up
testdrive](http://castrojo.wordpress.com/2010/03/11/using-testdrive-to-save-time-on-testing/)
and bang on it, file bugs, break stuff, post your emo mood on facebook,
flame someone on Twitter, or whatever.

[inspiration](http://listen.grooveshark.com/#/s/The+Great+Southern+Trendkill/lBCAl)
