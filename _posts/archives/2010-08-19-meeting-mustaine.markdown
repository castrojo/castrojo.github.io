This afternoon I met Dave Mustaine at the signing for his new
autobiography,
\_[Mustaine](http://www.amazon.com/Mustaine-Heavy-Metal-Memoir-Dave/dp/0061714372).
\_I was surprised (and elated) to see so many people there for a genre
that continually has been written off, even though it’s the greatest
form of music we as a species can ever hope to achieve.

This, my friends, is the face of pure joy. (Mine, not his, blame the
camera guy). More pics
[here](http://picasaweb.google.com/jorge.castro/MustaineBookSigning#).

[![](http://lh4.ggpht.com/_1Thkya8n93k/TG14f-3jNlI/AAAAAAAAdB8/kr6CX0IhBdw/s400/IMG_1966.JPG)](http://picasaweb.google.com/lh/photo/hatUS7MKVuL7gIiV1AnCdw?feat=embedwebsite)
