<div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">

Here’s a nice little gift from the X team, an [updated
drivers](https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-x/2009-April/000518.html)
PPA for those of you who want stable releases of drivers without
bleeding too much. Having been one of the folks bitten by Intel X driver
bugs this cycle I am glad we have this option available. I have been
wondering the past few months what just has been going on with this
driver since it has been very frustrating to watch it seemingly regress.
Keith Packard from Intel has blogged an
[update](http://keithp.com/blogs/Sharpening_the_Intel_Driver_Focus/) on
what’s going in -intel land which makes me feel a lot better.

Also, if you’re an nvidia user and want to help
[triage](https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-x/2009-April/000521.html)
some bugs that would be appreciated. Of course, the X team is always
looking for gifted people to fight the good fight, check out [the
wiki](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/X) find out how to help. Big thanks to
Bryce Harrington this cycle, who has been working dreadful hours and
soldiering on.
