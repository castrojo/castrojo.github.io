Jason Smith sends this along:

> So there was a [seemingly simple
> bug](https://bugs.launchpad.net/unity/+bug/683261), the launcher would
> autohide when the quicklist was open.This was due to the fact that the
> launcher was entirely unaware of when a quicklist was open.

![](http://media.tumblr.com/tumblr_ld6ihxZvhI1qb5bmy.png)

> So I layed out a design to follow in the [bug
> report](https://bugs.launchpad.net/unity/+bug/683261), essentially I
> left a stubbed class that someone could implement.

As it ends up Jamal Fanaian [has
returned](https://code.launchpad.net/~jamalta/unity/683261-autohide-quicklist)
for his 2nd straight day of Unity contributions. Jason continues:

> The class he created tracks the state of all quicklists in the
> launcher, then informs the launcher when they show/hide/change. He
> then made the launcher consume this information and avoid hiding when
> one was showing. Oh, and he cleaned up a bunch of code while he was
> doing it, just for giggles.

Not a bad start!
