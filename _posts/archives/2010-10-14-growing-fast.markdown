Ralph mentions [some of the
stats](http://drupal.txwikinger.me.uk/content/askubuntu-reaches-2000-questions-5000-answers-4000-users-20000-votes) from [askubuntu.com](http://askubuntu.com).
It’s been hectic trying to keep up with the grow, especially as the
stack method can be [confusing at
first](http://meta.askubuntu.com/questions/257/i-am-new-here-how-does-stackexchange-work).
Now that we’re out of beta I can go over some of the tools I’ve been
using to help best make use of the site.

The first is George Edison’s
[StackApplet](http://launchpad.net/stackapplet), a nice
appindicator-based reputation tracker with notify-osd support, so that
your growing reputation can motivate you throughout the day:

![](http://media.tumblr.com/tumblr_laav6tSka41qb5bmy.png)
