during my flight to FOSDEM), but I was surprised at how *thin* the book
is. A sysadmin book less than 800 pages? Ridiculous. Puppet has been
brought up on the Ars Technica forums over the last few months, so I
[started a
thread](http://episteme.arstechnica.com/eve/forums/a/tpc/f/96509133/m/888008550931).

Note: I think the book being so thin is a feature - a testament to a
well-designed tool. :)
