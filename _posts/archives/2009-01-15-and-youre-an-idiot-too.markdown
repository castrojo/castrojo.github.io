So yesterday/today Jill’s car was making a noise so we took it into the
mechanic. I don’t like seeing mechanics because my Dad is a “car guy”
and while I love cars as a whole, I don’t love cars as in I love to work
on them all the time. I can appreciate a 67 Stingray but I just don’t
spend weekends under the hood.

I hate mechanics because they ask you all these questions and get pissy
when you give them the wrong answer and they look at you like you don’t
deserve to drive a car. In fact, I am pretty sure there is an entire
Seinfeld episode about this.

Normal people use computers as a tool, I don’t understand why people
feel so empowered to lecture other people on how they should live their
lives, let alone how they should use their computer. I mean, my fridge
has a computer chip in it which runs something that is probably not free
software. I wonder who would dare tell me that I’m being oppressed by my
refrigerator.

Oh, nevermind.

Don’t worry [Cody](http://cody.zapto.org/?p=53), it get’s darkest before
it gets pitch black!
