Hear ye, hear ye!

As 13.04 rolls out the door so does a new version of Juju! We’ll be
conducting a Charm School to get everyone up to speed on Juju. Marco
Ceppi and myself will be conducting the class.

The event will be happening on [Ubuntu On Air](http://ubuntuonair.com)
via a Google Hangout. We’ll cover how to get started with Juju and how
to deploy services in a cloud, as well as an overview of what charms can
do for you. Everyone is welcome to participate, especially if you’re a
beginner!

1700 UTC (1200 EST) on Friday, May 3
------------------------------------

Here’s the [calendar
entry](https://www.google.com/calendar/render?eid=aGdwY2JtNWY5Y2llMGxuN3ExdnEwMXU4ZmcgajVxODVtbWk2dWp2anRpaTVzMW4zbGk1aW9AZw&ctz=Etc/GMT&sf=true&output=xml).
