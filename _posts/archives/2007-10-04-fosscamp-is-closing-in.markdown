<div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">

The first ever [FOSSCamp](http://fosscamp.org/) is just around the
corner, 27-28 October, Cambridge, USA. The list of
[attendees](http://fosscamp.org/HowToAttend) continues to grow,
including attendance by the impavid Joe Shaw.

We’ve gotten some great traction from our friends in upstream projects,
and we’re looking for more to make the event grow. If you’ve never
attended an “unconference”, it’s basically a freeform thing. People
discuss what they want, and just being around other people from other
projects enables this freeform discussion that goes where the attendees
want it to go.

Attendance is free and the invite is open to all hackers who want to
come work on open source stuff, so don’t forget to bring a friend. We’ll
see you all in Boston!
