<div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">

Rick Spencer’s [Quickly](http://launchpad.net/quickly) video tutorials
are now available in a more digestible form in the [Ubuntu Developer’s
Blip](http://ubuntudevelopers.blip.tv/) channel, which is syndicated to
the [youtube](http://www.youtube.com/user/ubuntudevelopers) channel of
the same name.

blip.tv allows us to post natively in ogg video, so check out episodes
[one](http://ubuntudevelopers.blip.tv/file/2730739?filename=Ubuntudevelopers-1_quickly_get_started192.ogv),
[two](http://ubuntudevelopers.blip.tv/file/2730740?filename=Ubuntudevelopers-2_quickly_signals_adding_keys329.ogv),
and
[three](http://ubuntudevelopers.blip.tv/file/2730774?filename=Ubuntudevelopers-3_quickly_pagcakging862.ogv).
To download directly just click the download link at the bottom.

Alan Pope and I have now set it up that native ogg videos will be
uploaded to the blip channels for both the developer channel and the
[screencasts](http://ubuntuscreencasts.blip.tv/) channel in native ogg.
Blip will syndicate it out to youtube and the Internet Archive, so
that’s useful. If you’re looking at making video content that is
relevant to Ubuntu people please let me know and I’ll help you get it
out there.
