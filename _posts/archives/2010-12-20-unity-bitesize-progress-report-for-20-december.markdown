It’s time for another Unity bitesize bug report. First off let’s welcome
another new contributor:

Stefano Candori, aka Cando. He’s worked on other projects, like the
GNOME Activity Journal, Emesene and Emesene 2.

![](http://media.tumblr.com/tumblr_ldqw0p9vwe1qb5bmy.png)

Cando has fixed: 

Bug 683466 - [There are 2 Quit menu options in
Quicklist](https://bugs.launchpad.net/unity/+bug/683466)

Which was the annoying thing where applications that generated menus
like Tomboy had had two two Quit Quit entries entries. See? I told you
it was annoying. He’s currently checking out adding [quick lists to the
Trash Can](https://bugs.launchpad.net/unity/+bug/688407). Unless of
course you are from England, in which case the correct term is Rubbish
Bin.

Here’s the interesting ones for the week. Feel free to grab any of these
bugs and [start hacking](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Unity/Bitesize).

-   Bug 688830 [Select quicklist items with just one right
    click](https://bugs.launchpad.net/unity/+bug/688830)

-   Bug 689929 [Update managers Apply Settings window doesn’t trigger
    unity’s smarthide](https://bugs.launchpad.net/unity/+bug/689929)

-   Bug 691114 [Quicklists for icons at the bottom of the screen are
    clipped by screen
    edge](https://bugs.launchpad.net/unity/+bug/691114)

-   Bug 677594 [Workspace switcher useless with one
    workspace](https://bugs.launchpad.net/unity/+bug/677594)

-   Bug 681428 [scrolling does not work on the sound
    menu](https://bugs.launchpad.net/unity/+bug/681428)

-   Bug 689010 [wrong icon for “blank cd-r disc” in
    launcher](https://bugs.launchpad.net/unity/+bug/689010)

-   Bug 683547 [Bottom launchers hard to expand with filled launcher
    bar, need edge
    scrolling](https://bugs.launchpad.net/unity/+bug/683547)

Here’s the [full list](http://goo.gl/tiheb).

**Other Unity News**

Reports are coming in about double window widgets and titles, don’t
worry, it’s just a transition while we debug the widgets in the top
panel (this is mostly fixed in trunk, expect a proper fix end of this
week):

![](http://media.tumblr.com/tumblr_ldqxqac9AC1qb5bmy.png)

This week also includes fixes from Neil Patel and Jason Smith, with some
other fixes from Michael Terry (a unity icon and a category in ccsm),
along with the return of launcher drag and drop of icons. (Check out the
picture)

![](http://i.imgur.com/pPqSc.png)

The team would also like to send a thanks to Omar Akram and Hernando
Torque for their ever vigilant bug work and testing.

Jason Smith ran a Unity [Ask Me Anything on
reddit](http://www.reddit.com/r/IAmA/comments/en0ti/i_am_an_ubuntu_unity_developer_ama/) if
you’re interested in some of the gory details of what it’s like to work
on Unity. You can also find more information on Unity from the
developers by following the [Unity
tag](http://askubuntu.com/questions/tagged/unity) on Ask Ubuntu.
