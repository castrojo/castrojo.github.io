GNOME is [looking for a
sysadmin](http://www.vuntz.net/journal/post/2010/06/02/Become-a)!

Don’t forget that you can support GNOME by becoming a [Friend of
GNOME](http://www.gnome.org/friends/)! One thing I learned from people
on the Board is that having a *monthly* subscription is more helpful to
the project. I used to give one lump sum every year, but that’s a bit
unpredictable.

When you sign up for a monthly subscription it makes things like having
a reliable source of income to the project on a more regular basis more
doable. This makes things like hiring a sysadmin easier to do.

So if you were like me and just randomly sending money once a year,
consider setting up a monthly donation spread out instead!
