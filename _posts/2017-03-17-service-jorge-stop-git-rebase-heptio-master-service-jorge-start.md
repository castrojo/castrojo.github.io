After 9 years, 20 Ubuntu releases, [14,455 edits](http://askubuntu.com/users/235/jorge-castro?tab=activity&sort=revisions) and 359,186 miles in the air, it's time for me to move on to something else. I've been privileged to work with some of the brightest people in the industry, and for that I'll always be grateful.

But I won't be going far, I'll be starting next month at [Heptio](http://heptio.com), where I will get to work with the in supporting and advancing [Kubernetes](http://kubernetes.io). The community is rapidly expanding, and I'm looking forward to contributing to the machinery that helps keep it a great place for people to participate. If you've not yet given k8s a spin, you should, it's great stuff and only getting better every release.

There are too many people who I'd like to thank, but you know who you are, and I'll still see everyone around at conferences. I'll be around for the next few weeks to tie things up, and then onward and upward!
